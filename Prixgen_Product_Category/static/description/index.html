Product Category

Prixgen Tech Solutions introduces product category functionality to differentiate between product, service, assets and charges by configuring accordingly at product master level and also helps while selecting the right combination at purchase and sales transaction .

List for selecting the product gets filtered based on the category selected at the line level, together with boolean option 'Can be purchased' being flagged true for purchase order and 'Can be sold' being flagged true for sales order.
